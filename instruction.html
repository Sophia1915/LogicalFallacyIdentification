Game Instructions

  • Given Statement: You will be presented with a statement that contains a logical fallacy.
  • First Attempt: Try to correctly identify the logical fallacy in the statement. If you can't recall a particular fallacy's name, click the “Show Fallacies” button in the top right corner for assistance. If your first guess is correct, you'll proceed to the next statement.
  • Second Attempt: If your first guess was incorrect, don't worry! You'll be given a multiple-choice question to help pinpoint the right fallacy.
  • Feedback: If you're unable to identify the fallacy on the second attempt, you'll receive an explanation detailing which fallacy was present and why.

Good luck and have fun sharpening your rhetorical skills!
